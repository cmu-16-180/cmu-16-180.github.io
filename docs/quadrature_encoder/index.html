How Quadrature works:

  • Channels A and B are physically offset by 90 electrical degrees.
  • When spinning Clockwise, Channel A leads B (A goes high, then B goes high).
  • When spinning Counter-Clockwise, Channel B leads A.
  • By comparing the previous state to the new state whenever a transition happens, the microcontroller knows whether to add or subtract from the pulse count!
  • The Z (Index) pulse fires exactly once per revolution to give an absolute zero position.
